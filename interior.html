About

This house is one of a few we have been working on in Mount Gretna . The first floor of his house had multiple levels in the existing flooring. The carpet and tile entryway were removed and the entire floor was raised up over 1" using layers of plywood. This brought everything to the same level height as the kitchen floor. New laminate flooring was installed throughout the entire area and new quarter round was installed. All the cabinet doors were trimmed to fit the new floor. The stairs were stripped of their carpet and cleaned and so was the hallway upstairs to reveal the original wood floors. All the Staples were removed throughout all the floors. The bathroom floor was also removed and new laminate was installed as well as a toilet and trim.
