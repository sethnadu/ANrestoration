About: Porch

If your balcony or porch is sagging and feels like it is going to fall off your house let us take care of it for you! We removed all the old and rotted wood from this one. We braced and reconstructed it and raised it up almost 6 inches to its original position. Then we built a corner brace to lock it in and support it. It feels as solid as a concrete floor now. We installed a new floor and secured the post back in place. Then we reinstalled the trim and soffit.

About: House

The front of this house was in dire need of repair. There was a lot of wind and water damage to the shingles and the Slate roofs. The wooden trim was rotting and falling apart and need to be completely stripped and repainted. The caulking around all the windows also needed redone. I replaced all the shingle roofs on the front and on the back of the house. We also fixed a bunch of rotted porch boards and replaced a bunch of siding that was blown off from the storm. I stripped and replaced any damaged wood and repainted all the wooden trim work. The shutters were rotted so I made custom shutters out of beadboard to match the detail on the top of the upper window. The shutters are made of PVC so they will never rot. I also painted the trim work on the house next door as well.
